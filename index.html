LiveToDoGood Foundation's Mission is to help people do good, through promoting good and supporting empowerment initiatives globally.

Our Mission

Help People Do Good

To create a good world together for the world is the sum of our collective good.

Our beliefs

Do Good
Create Good
Feel Good

We believe that if we live to do good, we will live a happy and meaningful life and in the process create a good world.

Our Activities

Promoting Good

  • Sharing information on how we can do good through different media.
  • Promote more peace and understanding across different faiths and nations.

Supporting Empowerment Initiatives

  • Relief - to help improve the health and lives of people affected by emergency situations.
  • Development - to help provide access to resource to enable people to do the most good.

We are just getting started. A lot more to follow in the future. Please stay tuned and let’s LiveToDoGood!
