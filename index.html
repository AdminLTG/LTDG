About Us

Our Mission Help people do good
  
Our Beliefs Doing good is the path to creating a good world and to live a happy and purposeful life

1) We believe that if each of us does good, we will collectively create a good world, as the world is the sum of all our actions.
2) We also believe a life spent doin good is one of the most fulfilling, meaningful and happy ways to live and to find and share peace with others.
Our Strategy Promoting good and supporting empowerment initiatives globally

1) Promote good to help people live happy, fulfilling, peaceful and good lives through sharing information & inspiration.
2) Support empowerment initiatives globally to help people make the most of their lives and do good.
Our Activities
Promoting good

1) Sharing Information and inspiration and guidance through different media on why do good, what it means to do good, how we can all do most good.
2) Through our fund LIFH Impact - working with corporates to encourage them to do more good by actively engaging with all their stakeholders.
3) To promote more peace and understanding across different faiths through different initiatives.

 Empowerment initiatives

1) Relief - where we seek to help provide immediate relief from suffering in emergency situations and to help provide access to basic needs - water, food and healthcare.
2) Development - where we are looking to build a social impact fund to empower entrepreneurs to use market forces to do good, and also looking to support initiatives that provide access to free and high quality education to people.
